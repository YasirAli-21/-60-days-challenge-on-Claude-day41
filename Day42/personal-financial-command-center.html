10 — Habits

Tips, Checklist & Resources

Short, practical guidance for building strong money habits as a student.

Tips for students on a fixed allowance

  • Log expenses the same day — memory fades fast with cash spending.
  • Give every rupee of your allowance a job: needs, wants, savings.
  • Set aside savings first, right when allowance arrives, not what's left over.
  • Track subscriptions and small recurring costs — they add up quietly.
  • Review your budget vs. actual spend weekly, not just at month end.

Resources to explore
  • State Bank of Pakistan's financial literacy materials for young savers.
  • Campus career or finance clubs — many run budgeting workshops for students.
  • Basic personal finance books on budgeting, saving, and avoiding debt.
  • University career office resources on managing stipends and part-time pay.
